About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineer and MBA graduate from NMIMS MPSTME, Mumbai — one of India's premier engineering and management institutions. My journey spans the intersection of engineering precision and business strategy.

My passion lies in the full product development cycle: from the initial spark of an idea, through rigorous design and prototyping, to real-world testing and iteration. I thrive in environments where technical depth meets creative problem-solving.

As an automobile enthusiast, I'm drawn to the mechanical complexity and design language of vehicles. Whether it's the thermodynamics of an engine or the aerodynamics of a chassis, I find beauty in the science of motion.
